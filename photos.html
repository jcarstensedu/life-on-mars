This is Spoke, she came to us from a small shelter in norther Colorado. As a puppy Spoke was timid, shy, scared of everthing but now after a year of growth she is full of life and love. Spoke is a renagade when shes in a playfull mood and a jelly ball when she is tired. She draws insperation from the morning and makes for a great alarm clock. I was unsure how living with a dog in a small van with another person would go, but its been great. Thought we have had our ups and downs, having this rambunchis fure ball has been a blessing. Spoke is indapendent, smart and loves her own space. This of course means that we can leave her in the van alone,with plenty of water and ideal conditions, it is her home after all , and she wont bark,wine, or fuss at all. This also means that she has additude and acts like a drama queen when she is comfertable on the bed. So much so that she will give a huff of a bark, jult up and move herself if disturebed.
The photo that starts this collom was taken at the Mallino Canyon pull out just past mile marker 4 on the right of the rode as you assend up My. Lemmon in Tucson, AZ. The jurney up this canyon is breath taking. as you approch you are greated with Giant Sagoia pebbaling the left and right shoulders, a large sky that seems ever vast and blue, and striped granit stone on the herizone. Soon you are in gulfed in the rocky spires, cactus and open skys. Winding your way up into the belly of the canyon. Black top strething for miles, you could drive for ever in these hills. A certan peace resides here, a beauty somehow untouched.
